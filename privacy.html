Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Steam Page Check ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information.

Overview (Plain English)

Steam Page Check is a client-side web tool. All text analysis happens entirely in your browser. Your content is never uploaded to our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not:

  • Upload, store, or process your text on a server
  • Collect personal information like name, address, or phone number
  • Use analytics, trackers, or advertising cookies
  • Track your usage behavior

Your store page content remains on your device at all times.

Local Processing

When you use the App to check your Steam store page text:

  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • No text is transmitted over the internet
  • Closing the page ends the session

Local Storage

The App uses your browser's localStorage only to:

  • Remember whether you have unlocked premium features
  • Remember your theme preference (light/dark)

This data stays on your device. Clearing browser data will remove this information.

Payments

Payments are handled entirely by Paddle, our payment processor and Merchant of Record.

We do not:

  • See your payment details
  • Store credit card or billing information

Paddle may collect personal and payment information as required to process transactions and taxes. Please refer to Paddle's Privacy Policy for details.

Third-Party Services

The App relies on:

  • Paddle – payment processing
  • Vercel – website hosting

No other third-party services receive your data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
