Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Steam Page Check handles your information.

Overview (Plain English)

Steam Page Check is a client-side web tool. All text analysis happens entirely in your browser. Your content is never uploaded to our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any store page text you paste into the tool
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not have user accounts or require registration

Information We May Collect

If you purchase a license to unlock premium features:

  • Your payment is processed by Paddle, our payment processor and Merchant of Record
  • Paddle collects your payment details and email address
  • We do not have access to your full payment details

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember your unlock status. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Hosting

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, access times) as part of normal web hosting operations.

Third Parties

  • Paddle — Payment processing. See Paddle's Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Hosting. See Vercel's Privacy Policy

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
